Our Story
From Napoli to Surry Hills

Marco Esposito opened Trattoria da Marco in 2009 after leaving his family's restaurant in Napoli. He brought with him his grandmother's recipes, a philosophy of simplicity, and an obsession with sourcing the right ingredients.

Our wine list is curated exclusively by Godot Wines Australia — a boutique importer specialising in small Italian producers. Every bottle on our list can be delivered to your home.
